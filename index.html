Meet the Team

FinBean Media is led by Suyash Maheshwari, a financial journalist with over 7 years of experience covering markets, business, and the economy. He has worked with some of India’s top newsrooms, anchored stock market shows in both English and Hindi, and contributed to investor education initiatives for financial platforms.

He’s backed by a small, agile team of journalists, digital marketers, video editors, and graphic designers — all focused on one goal: helping financial brands become credible, content-led destinations. From simplifying market news to designing thumb-stopping creatives, we function as an extension of your brand, speaking in your voice — not ours.
